CalTri Fitness Android Application

Usage:

- Lightweight, ad-free triathlon training tracker.
- Record training sessions - cycle, swim or run.
- Add name, date, distance, time, comments and workout intensity to each entry with record screen.
- All entries are stored on the Training Log screen for future viewing.
- Check monthly progress with a visual representation on the Graph Screen.
- Sync - Save a copy of your training log via email in .csv format for future viewing.
